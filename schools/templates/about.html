About this project

Getting into a good primary school at a nearby location is top priority for most young couples with children in Singapore. In this project, we seek to create geographic information system in the form of a map-based app that will contain information about the location of kindergartens and primary schools which will help parents in the process of kindergarten and primary school selection.
